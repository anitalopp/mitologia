What is Greek World?

Welcome to Greek World! your digital gateway to explore the wonders and mysteries of the fascinating universe of Greek mythology.
Immerse yourself in an epic journey through timeless legends, powerful gods, and brave heroes that have shaped the rich cultural heritage of ancient Greece.
We offer you a comprehensive view of Greek myths, from classical stories to lesser-known tales, presented in an accessible manner for everyone. Discover the creation of the world according to Greek mythology, from the primordial Chaos to the Titanomachy and the rise of the Olympians.
Explore the genealogy of the Olympian gods, from the mighty Zeus, the king of gods, to the wise Athena and the loving Aphrodite. Learn about legendary heroes like Achilles, Perseus, and Heracles, whose brave feats have left an indelible mark on mythology and Western culture.
Our website provides informative articles, detailed narratives, and stunning visual representations that will transport you to ancient Greece. Additionally, you will find sections dedicated to the influence of Greek mythology on art, literature, and philosophy, showcasing how these myths have endured through the centuries.
For those seeking an interactive experience, we offer games and quizzes that will test your knowledge of Greek mythology. Immerse yourself in the richness of Hellenic culture through our carefully curated resources, recommended books, and links to archaeological sites that will allow you to delve deeper into the legacy of ancient Greece.
At Greek World, we strive to be your reliable and exciting source of information about Greek mythology. Join us on this unique journey through myths and legends that have captured the imagination of generations, and discover why Greek mythology remains an endless source of inspiration and knowledge. Welcome to our Greek world!
